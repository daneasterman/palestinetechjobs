Why Palestine Tech Jobs?

As we enter the 2020s, the end of the occupation and a just resolution to the Israel-Palestinian conflict appears further away than ever. Political change and progress for the Palestinian people has ground to a halt.

And yet, the world is not standing still. While we cannot easily force our politicians to make the political changes needed, it is very literally in our hands to change the Palestinian economic situation for the better.

Technology has become the most dynamic economic force around the world, and in Palestine this is no exception. We believe that the growth of the startup sector, high-paying tech jobs and the knowledge economy provide the best path for the Palestinians to economic prosperity.

Palestine Tech Jobs was founded on this basis.

Why focus on remote jobs?

Put simply, to parapharse the science fiction writer William Gibson: remote work is the future, it just hasn't fully arrived yet. Freedom of movement has been obviously been denied to Palestinians for a long time now, but technology and knowledge work can help to bypass these physical limitations.

Working with international companies can offer much higher wages than are currently available in Palestine. But these opportunities can also give tech workers valuable real-world experience they can use to put back into Palestine's start-up economy.
